Why consultancy?

It's becoming increasingly clear that artificial intelligence is the technology of today and tomorrow. However, according to a 2025 MIT study, 95% of companies investing in AI fail to see a return on their investment. This is because companies cannot properly integrate existing technologies into their systems. As someone who has been directly involved in the development process of these models, I understand the strengths and weaknesses of these models and I am confident that I can guide your company in this regard.

Why do I do this?

My consultancy is non-profit. My main goal is to contribute to the development of Turkey's technology ecosystem and thus to the development of our country.

Fees

The first two 45-minute sessions with any company are free of charge. If the consulting company deems it appropriate, an appropriate amount can be donated to the Foundation for the Support of Contemporary Life.

Partnership

If you would like to establish a regular consultancy relationship, please contact me; I would be happy to start the process if we reach a mutual agreement.
